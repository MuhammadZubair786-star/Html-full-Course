Seo & Core Web Vitals in Html

  1. LCP
  2. FID
  3. CLS
LCP

Dikhne mein tez (Fast to see) - Content jaldi load ho (Content should load quickly).

FID

Karne mein tez (Fast to do) - Clicks par jawab jaldi dein (Respond quickly to clicks).

CLS

Lage raho jahan ho (Stay where you are) - Content idhar-udhar na ho (Content shouldn't jump around).
